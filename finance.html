Last reviewed01 May 2026
ClassificationInternal — Controlled
Applies toAll revenue-impacting approvals

This matrix is the authoritative control for discount and commercial-term approvals across Meridian Cloud. It supersedes any conflicting approval thresholds stated in departmental playbooks.

Discount approval thresholds

Discount off listRequired approverSLA for approval
0% – 15%Account Executive (self-approve)Immediate
Above 15% up to 30%Deal Desk (Finance)1 business day
Above 30% up to 45%Deal Desk + VP Sales2 business days
Above 45%CFO3 business days

Authority note

Any discount above 15% requires Deal Desk sign-off before the quote is sent to the customer. Self-approval authority for Account Executives ends at 15%. Where a departmental playbook states a higher self-approval limit, this matrix governs.

Other controlled terms

  • Non-standard payment terms beyond Net 30 require Deal Desk review.
  • Custom SLAs or liability caps require Legal and Deal Desk review.

Employee Expense Policy

Version3.0
Effective date01 January 2026
Last reviewed01 January 2026
ClassificationInternal
Applies toAll employees

Rules for incurring and claiming business expenses.

General principles

  • Expenses must be reasonable, necessary, and business-related.
  • Submit claims within 30 days of the expense date.
  • Receipts are required for any single expense above $25.

Travel

  • Book economy class for flights under six hours.
  • Pre-approval required for any trip with total cost above $2,000.

Revenue Recognition Guidelines

Document ownerFinance — Controller
Version2.1
Effective date01 February 2026
Last reviewed01 February 2026
ClassificationInternal — Controlled
Applies toFinance, RevOps

Summary of how Meridian Cloud recognizes subscription revenue under the applicable standard.

Core rules

  • Subscription revenue is recognized ratably over the contract term.
  • Setup fees are recognized over the expected customer life.
  • Usage-based charges are recognized in the period the usage occurs.
