Sales Playbook: Discounting & Deal Approval

Document ownerSales Operations
Version3.2
Effective date15 April 2026
Last reviewed15 April 2026
ClassificationInternal
Applies toAll Account Executives, Sales Managers

This playbook gives Account Executives (AEs) practical guidance on structuring deals and applying discounts to close business efficiently while protecting margin.

Standard discount authority

To keep deal velocity high, Account Executives are empowered to make discounting decisions without escalation within their delegated authority.

  • Account Executives may approve discounts of up to 25% off list price on their own authority, without escalation.
  • Discounts above 25% require Sales Manager approval.
  • Discounts above 40% require VP of Sales approval.

Best practices

  • Anchor on value, not price. Lead with ROI before discussing discounts.
  • Trade concessions — ask for a longer term or case-study rights in exchange for a discount.
  • Record the discount rationale in the CRM opportunity notes for every deal.

Related documents

For tier pricing see the Pricing Tiers reference maintained by Finance. For multi-year and non-standard terms, consult the Quote-to-Cash Process Overview.
