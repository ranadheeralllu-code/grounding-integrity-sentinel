Support Tier Definitions

Document ownerCustomer Support
Version2.0
Effective date01 April 2026
Last reviewed01 April 2026
ClassificationInternal — may be shared with customers
Applies toSupport, Sales

Describes what each support tier includes. Specific contractual response times for Enterprise customers are set in the signed Master Services Agreement.

Tiers

  • Standard: business-hours support, response targets per plan.
  • Enterprise: 24x7 support for critical incidents, with contractual SLAs defined in the MSA.

Where an internal runbook and a signed customer agreement state different response times, the signed agreement governs the commitment to the customer.
